The Perfect PB&J

Description

This is a recipe for a PB&J

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Jelly
  • Peanut Butter
  • Butter Knife
  • Sharp Knife
  • Cutting Board

Directions

  1. Lay the bread out
  2. Put the PB on one slice of bread with the butter knife
  3. Put the jelly on the other slice of bread with the butter knife
  4. Put the two slices of bread together
  5. Cut the sandwich in half on the cutting board with the sharp knife
  6. Enjoy!
